# ReadRoom — Orynth submission package

## Product name
ReadRoom

## Tagline
Read the room before you post.

## One-line description
AI audience simulation that shows you how your words may be interpreted—before you publish them.

## Short description
ReadRoom is a perception-intelligence platform for creators, founders, and professionals. Paste a
post, caption, email, or announcement; choose the room you're speaking to; and get a transparent
simulation of how supporters, skeptics, customers, and partners may read it—plus rewrites that keep
your voice.

## Long description
Most tools tell you what to say. ReadRoom tells you how it will be heard.

Paste something you're about to publish, pick the audience "room" it's entering, and ReadRoom
simulates the reception: a Room Score, seven dimensions (clarity, trust, authority, warmth,
originality, platform fit, polarisation), five distinct audience perspectives, misreading and
out-of-context "clip" risks, your strongest and weakest sentence, and three rewrites—clearer,
sharper, safer—that preserve your voice instead of flattening it.

Build **Audience Rooms** for the people who matter (customers, investors, skeptics, partners), a
**Voiceprint** so recommendations still sound like you, and run a **Profile Audit** to see what your
bio and recent posts actually communicate. Everything is private by default; nothing is ever posted
on your behalf.

ReadRoom is a simulation, not a truth detector. It never predicts exact engagement, never diagnoses
people, and never claims to know what any individual will think. It just gives you a second
perspective before your words leave your hands.

## Core features
- **Quick Read** — a structured, editorial report on how content may land.
- **Audience Rooms** — save the audiences you speak to, with their values and objections.
- **Voiceprint** — a writing profile that keeps rewrites unmistakably yours.
- **Profile Audit** — credibility, clarity, positioning, and memorability, with a 7-day plan.
- **Compare** — put 2–4 variants side by side and see which lands and which gets misread.
- **Shareable reports** — polished, privacy-safe share cards (original text hidden by default).

## Product differentiation
- **Perception, not generation.** ReadRoom analyses reception; it doesn't write over your voice.
- **Audience-specific.** The same words are simulated across distinct rooms, not scored in the abstract.
- **Uncertainty-aware.** Confidence and explicit assumptions are shown, never hidden certainty.
- **Privacy-first.** Nothing is shared or posted without an explicit, revocable action.

## AI and privacy disclaimer
ReadRoom is an audience-perception simulation powered by a configurable, vision-capable Claude model.
It does not predict exact engagement, does not verify facts, and does not diagnose people or infer
personal characteristics. Content is processed server-side; keys never reach the browser, and raw
private content is not logged in production by default. Analyses are private until you explicitly
share them.

## Suggested FAQ answers
**Is this an AI writer?** No. It shows you how your writing may be interpreted and offers optional
rewrites that keep your voice.

**Will it make me sound generic?** The opposite is the goal—your Voiceprint preserves your rhythm,
vocabulary, and the lines you refuse to soften.

**Can it predict if my post will go viral?** No, and it won't pretend to. It simulates interpretation,
not engagement.

**Is my content private?** Yes. Private by default; share links are opt-in, hide your original text
unless you choose to reveal it, and can be revoked anytime.

**What does it cost?** Free public beta—five reads per day. Paid plans may come later; nothing will be
charged without explicit consent.
